Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Introduction

Welcome to Cres.

Cres is a personal growth application that brings together Notes, Checklists, Tasks, Habits, Mood, and Vision Boards in one calm workspace.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, how we protect it, and the choices available to you regarding your data.

By using Cres, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we may collect:

  • Email address
  • Authentication provider (Apple Sign In, Google Sign In, or Email & Password)
  • Country or region, if you choose to provide it

We do not require your real name or profile photo.

Content You Create

Cres is designed to store information you choose to create and manage within the app.

This may include:

  • Notes
  • Hidden Notes
  • Checklists
  • Hidden Checklists
  • Tasks
  • Habits
  • Mood entries
  • Vision Boards
  • Folders and organization data
  • Photos and attachments
  • Personal reflections and goals

Your content remains associated with your account and is used solely to provide the functionality of the service.

Information Stored Locally

To support offline functionality, Cres may store certain information on your device.

When connectivity becomes available, data may synchronize with our cloud services.

How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Authenticate your identity
  • Save and synchronize your data
  • Restore your information across devices
  • Provide application features
  • Improve reliability, performance, and security
  • Respond to support requests
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell personal information.

We do not rent personal information.

We do not use your Notes, Checklists, Tasks, Habits, Mood entries, Vision Boards, or other personal content for advertising purposes.

Biometric Authentication

Cres may allow you to use Face ID, Touch ID, or other device-supported biometric authentication to unlock protected content.

Protected content may include:

  • Hidden Notes
  • Hidden Checklists
  • App Lock protected areas

Biometric authentication is handled entirely by your device and operating system.

Cres does not receive, collect, store, or process biometric information, facial scans, fingerprints, or biometric templates.

Authentication

Cres may allow account access through:

  • Apple Sign In
  • Google Sign In
  • Email and Password Authentication

Authentication is provided through secure third-party services.

We never receive your Apple account password or Google account password.

Security

We take reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

Security measures may include:

  • Secure authentication systems
  • Encrypted communications
  • Access controls
  • Cloud security protections
  • Device-level security features

However, no method of transmission or storage can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Data Retention

We retain information for as long as necessary to provide Cres and fulfill legitimate business or legal obligations.

If you delete content, it may be removed from active systems within a reasonable period.

If you delete your account, associated personal information and user-generated content may be permanently removed, subject to legal, operational, and backup requirements.

Account Deletion

You may permanently delete your account directly within Cres.

When your account is deleted, associated data may also be deleted, including:

  • Notes
  • Hidden Notes
  • Checklists
  • Hidden Checklists
  • Tasks
  • Habits
  • Mood entries
  • Vision Boards
  • Photos and attachments
  • Settings and preferences

Deletion is intended to be permanent and may not be reversible.

Third-Party Services

Cres may rely on trusted third-party providers to operate and improve the service.

These providers may include:

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Firebase Firestore
  • Firebase Storage
  • Firebase App Check
  • Apple Sign In
  • Google Sign In

These providers process information only as necessary to provide their services.

Children’s Privacy

Cres is not intended for children under the age of 13 or the minimum age required by local law.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If we learn that personal information has been collected from a child without appropriate consent, we will take reasonable steps to remove it.

International Users

Your information may be processed and stored in countries other than your own.

By using Cres, you acknowledge that information may be transferred and processed in accordance with this Privacy Policy and applicable laws.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal information, including:

  • Accessing your information
  • Correcting inaccurate information
  • Requesting deletion of information
  • Restricting certain processing activities
  • Receiving a copy of your information

To exercise these rights, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

When changes are made, the “Last Updated” date will be revised.

Continued use of Cres after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
